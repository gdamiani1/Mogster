06 / PRINCIPLES

RULES FOR
EVERY SCREEN.

PRINCIPLE 01
GRAIN ON EVERYTHING

Every dark surface gets an SVG noise overlay at 5-7% opacity. Never flat solid colors. Atmosphere > cleanliness.

PRINCIPLE 02
BIG NUMBERS, TINY LABELS

Score = 180pt Anton. Label "AURA" = 10pt mono. Hierarchy through contrast of scale, not weight.

PRINCIPLE 03
CROP MARKS + STAMPS

Registration marks in card corners. Rotated hazard stamps for paths. Issue numbers + dates in tiny mono. Print-shop aesthetic.

PRINCIPLE 04
MOTION SERVES DRAMA

Not micro-interactions on hover. Big cinematic reveals: score ticks up, stats fill in sequence, tier label lands with a THUD. Boxing announcer vibes.

PRINCIPLE 05
MONO FOR DATA, DISPLAY FOR EMOTION

Stats, scores, timestamps, metadata = JetBrains Mono. Tier names, roasts, headlines = Anton. Two type systems, crystal clear roles.

PRINCIPLE 06
YELLOW IS EARNED

Hazard yellow is the primary accent but use it sparingly: CTAs, high scores, active states, winner reveals. Scarcity makes it powerful.
